Welcome to AFM BOKSBURG Church

A BRIEF HISTORY & BACKGROUND

AFM Boksburg started from AFM Kempton park through the vision of senior Pastor T Munemo. The senior pastor had a burden to start a cell group called Benoni Boksburg which was a unity of the city of Boksburg and the city of Benoni. The Cell group was started in 2012 and Mr and Mrs Mukumba senior were elected as the leaders of the cell group and services were being held at their residency 107 Malbore court, Atlasview.

The cell group grew and they had to change the venue because the numbers had out grown the venue and in the same year the senior pastor decided to split the cell group and Boksburg became an independent cell group. The group still out grew all the venues they found until they moved to an AFM church in Boksburg.

In 2013 the cell group had moved to comet Primary school with Mr and Mrs Marume as the the cell leaders and in the same year as the work was growing the senior Pastor decided to try Sunday services that would start at 8:30am and people would go to Kempton afterwards. In their first Sunday service as a cell group there was more than 50 people in attendance. The Sunday services started to grow and the Pastor T Munemo and his team decide to consider Boksburg as an assembly. A new structure was appointed and Deacon Ndlovu was appointed as the deputy Chair Person of the Assembly, Mr P Simangorwa was elected the secretary and Mr P F Mapingure was elected the church administrator.

The work grew and the people could no longer fit in the classroom at comet primary school so the church was moved to a hotel hall in Boksburg North where they attended for a small period and from there they went to a church building at 72 Charl Cilliers Boksburg north.

In February 2017 the church appointed Pastor F.N Mazivanhanga as their local Pastor and around the end of March by the grace of God a door opened for the church to move to a bigger venue at Freeway Bible Church, Corner Doone and Rondebult roads and to date the services are being held at the same venue.

Currently the membership of the church is over 500 members and there is still so much growth happening by the grace of God.
