Get to know about AFM Boksburg Church

Who is Welcomed

Whether you are new on this Christian journey and desire to have your faith strengthened or you are searching to know God more intimately, we anticipate getting to know you here at AFM Boksburg Church.

Sunday Gatherings

Common Questions

Who is Our Pastor?
Pastor F.N Mazivanhanga.
Serving Ministries?
Men`s fellowship, Ladies, Youth, Sunday School, Ushering, Hospitality and Decorations, Choir, Praise and Worship.

What to expect

We are a multicultural congregation that welcomes every nationality and race. We offer each member of your family a place to connect with friends. Here you can learn and be encouraged with relevant teaching and anointed preaching. We are confident that you will find each service to be refreshing and uplifting.

Get connected

Currently the membership of the church is over 500 members and there is still so much growth happening by the grace of God. JOIN A CELL GROUPS [ Boksburg North, Boksburg East, Boksburg West, Impala Park, Windmill Park, Diaspora ]

About our church

Our mission at AFM Boksburg is to have the gospel of Jesus Christ preached to all people, to depopulate the kingdom of Satan and ensure that many people come to Christ.
